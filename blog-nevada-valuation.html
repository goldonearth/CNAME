How to Value a Gold Mine in Nevada (2026 Guide)

Complete Guide to Nevada Gold Mine Valuation

Understanding Nevada Gold Mine Valuation

Nevada produces over 70% of all gold mined in the United States and ranks as the world's 4th largest gold-producing region. Valuing a Nevada gold mine requires understanding the state's unique geology, regulatory environment, and market dynamics.

Key Valuation Factors

  • Reserve Size and Grade: Proven & Probable reserves, cut-off grade, strip ratio
  • Infrastructure: Power access, water rights, road access, processing facilities
  • Permitting Status: BLM Plan of Operations, NDEP permits, water rights
  • Market Comparables: Recent Nevada transactions, per-ounce valuations
  • Net Present Value: DCF analysis with appropriate discount rates

Nevada Valuation Benchmarks (2026)

Current Market Values:
• Producing mines: $800-$2,000 per proven ounce
• Advanced development: $200-$500 per indicated ounce
• Exploration properties: $50-$150 per inferred ounce
• Greenfield exploration: $100-$500 per acre
