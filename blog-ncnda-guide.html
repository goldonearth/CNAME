Understanding NCNDA Agreements in Mining Transactions

Complete Guide to Non-Circumvention, Non-Disclosure Agreements

What is an NCNDA?

NCNDA stands for Non-Circumvention, Non-Disclosure Agreement. It's a legally binding contract that protects two critical things: your confidential information and your business relationships.

Why NCNDAs Matter in Mining

  • Protects Proprietary Data: Geological surveys, assay reports, production costs
  • Prevents Circumvention: Buyers can't bypass intermediaries
  • Establishes Professionalism: Serious buyers always use NCNDAs

5 Key Components of a Proper NCNDA

  1. Definition of Confidential Information: Clearly specifies what's protected
  2. Non-Circumvention Clause: Prevents parties from bypassing each other (2-5 years)
  3. Term and Termination: How long the agreement lasts
  4. Jurisdiction: Which country/state's laws govern the agreement
  5. Remedies for Breach: Legal consequences if violated
